## Summary

This source is the chronological progress log for the Clash95 HD mod, covering
the movement from early menu/input probes through no-popup CDB validation,
current HD map evidence gates, right-bottom UI investigation, and castle
barracks centering work. [source: docs/hd/HD_MOD_PROGRESS.md]

## Durable Facts

- The progress log records a current repo-only HD map smoke matrix with overall
  `PASS`, including patch-stage proof and post-owner evidence proof. [source:
  docs/hd/HD_MOD_PROGRESS.md]
- The progress log records the evidence-index interpretation that the current
  HD map path draws right/bottom cells when visibility permits, so normal black
  cells in the save are explained by fog/unexplored state. [source:
  docs/hd/HD_MOD_PROGRESS.md]
- The progress log treats right-bottom UI and bottom-tooltip recovery as a
  separate UI composition problem rather than another map-loop widening problem.

- The May 11 entries document castle/barracks centered display, centered input,
  descriptor hitbox, action descriptor, interior catalog, and success-branch
  proof runs. [source: docs/hd/HD_MOD_PROGRESS.md]
